From Humming to Harmony: My Journey into Singing

From a young age, we all catch a glimpse of who we are, our talents, and our capabilities. For me, it became clear early on that I had a gift for singing, and it's something I've cherished about myself ever since. I come from a musical family, surrounded by talented siblings, aunts, and uncles who share the same love for music. While my musical journey began at home, it was in the church where I decided to do something about my voice. The spiritual and communal atmosphere of the church became the perfect backdrop for me to explore and develop my passion for singing.
